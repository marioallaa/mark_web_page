Who's Mark?

Mark is a Personal Assistant that can interact with your Smart Things, control your home, recognize people at the door, adapt to your routine and a lot more while making your home more energy efficient, your life easier and giving you a more human like experience.

Prof of concept

Below you will find interviews that explain what "Mark" does and where Mark's development process is.

Fully Secured

Whats the need of an virtual assistant if everybody can get your data?
Mark can be located on the cloud or we can create a server on your own home so you know all the time where your data are. Both of those options will be protected with End to End Encryption to guarantee a better security.

Smart living

Mark can be installed in your home or office and make your life easier by doing the most simple actions such as closing the windows, turning on or off the lights, adjusting the temperature, opening the door to friends and family, lock down the building in case of a break in, notify the police and a lot more..

Where's the AI?

Marks core is a Chat Bot that learns by your every action, he can talk to you, chat with you, and even joke with you. He is fully personalized by you, because mark records every single use and tries to predict your routine, so that you won't have to bother to ask him something.

Easy To Access

While you are at home you can just talk with him. When you go out or when you are at work you can open the application, or talk with him through messaging apps such as messenger.
